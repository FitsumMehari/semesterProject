1. Getting Started

To begin, open your web browser and go to www.yourwebsite.com. Make sure you have a stable internet connection.

2. Creating an Account

Click on the “Sign Up” button on the homepage. Fill in your personal details, including your name, email, and password. Verify your email address by clicking on the link sent to your inbox. Log in using your new credentials.
